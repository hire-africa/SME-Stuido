# Professional Business Proposal Template - Updated

## Overview

The business proposal template has been completely redesigned to be a comprehensive, professional document suitable for large companies and enterprise-level proposals. It now includes real business content instead of placeholder text.

## Template Structure

### Page 1: Cover Page
- **Business Proposal** title with gold accent
- Client company name
- Current date
- Professional dark background with decorative elements

### Page 2: Executive Summary
- Overview of the proposal
- Business context and approach
- Key highlights and strategic points
- Professional introduction to the engagement

### Page 3: Scope of Work
- **Phase 1: Discovery & Planning**
  - Needs assessment and stakeholder interviews
  - Strategic roadmap development
  - Resource allocation and timeline

- **Phase 2: Implementation**
  - Project execution and deliverables
  - Quality assurance checkpoints
  - Stakeholder collaboration

- **Phase 3: Deployment & Support**
  - Testing and optimization
  - Training and documentation
  - Ongoing support services

### Page 4: Investment & Timeline
- **Investment Summary**
  - Project Delivery costs (60%)
  - Support & Training (25%)
  - Contingency (10%)
  - Total Investment (highlighted)

- **Project Timeline**
  - Week 1-2: Discovery phase
  - Week 3-8: Implementation phase
  - Week 9-10: Deployment phase

### Page 5: About [Company Name]
- Organization details
- Business type and sector
- Target market information
- Business overview and strategic objectives

### Page 6: Thank You Page
- Professional closing
- Company name
- Decorative elements

## Data Integration

The template automatically fills with user-provided data:

```typescript
{
  businessName: "Company Name",
  businessType: "Industry Type",
  sector: "Business Sector",
  description: "Company description",
  targetMarket: "Target market",
  monthlyRevenue: 500000,
  fundingNeeded: 2500000,
  fundingPurpose: "Strategic objectives"
}
```

## Financial Calculations

Investment breakdown is automatically calculated:
- **Project Delivery**: 60% of funding needed
- **Support & Training**: 25% of funding needed
- **Contingency**: 15% of funding needed
- **Total**: Sum of all components

Example: If funding needed is MK 2,500,000:
- Project Delivery: MK 1,500,000
- Support & Training: MK 625,000
- Contingency: MK 375,000
- **Total: MK 2,500,000**
