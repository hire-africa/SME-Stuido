# PowerPoint Export - Quick Start Guide

## What Changed?

Your **Company Profile** and **Pitch Deck** generators now export as **professional PowerPoint presentations (.pptx)** instead of Word documents, with beautiful master slides and custom templates.

## How to Use

### 1. Generate a Pitch Deck
```
1. Go to: /generate/pitch-deck
2. Fill in your business details:
   - Business Name
   - Tagline
   - Problem Statement
   - Solution
   - Target Market
   - Market Size
   - Business Model
   - Funding Amount
   - Use of Funds
3. Click "Generate Pitch Deck"
4. Review the AI-generated content
5. Click "Download as Professional Slides (.pptx)"
6. Open in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LibreOffice
```

### 2. Generate a Company Profile
```
1. Go to: /generate/company-profile
2. Fill in your company details:
   - Business Name
   - Business Type
   - Sector
   - Years in Operation
   - Team Size
   - Services (add multiple)
   - Key Achievements (add multiple)
3. Click "Generate Company Profile"
4. Review the AI-generated content
5. Click "Download as Professional Slides (.pptx)"
6. Open in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LibreOffice
```

## What You Get

### Pitch Deck Presentation
- **Slide 1**: Title slide with your business name and professional branding
- **Slides 2-13**: Content slides with AI-generated pitch content
- **Slide 14**: "Thank You" closing slide
- **Features**: Page numbers, accent bars, professional color scheme

### Company Profile Presentation
- **Slide 1**: Cover slide with company name
- **Slides 2-11**: Section slides with company information
- **Features**: Page numbers, header bars, professional formatting

## File Format

- **Format**: PowerPoint (.pptx)
- **Compatible with**: Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, LibreOffice Impress
- **File naming**: `[BusinessName]_[type]_[date].pptx`
  - Example: `TechStart_Solutions_pitch_deck_2024-11-18.pptx`

## Tips for Best Results

## Troubleshooting

**Download not working?**
- Check your browser's download settings
- Try a different browser
- Check internet connection

**File won't open?**
- Ensure you have PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LibreOffice installed
- Try opening with "Open with..." option
- Download again if file is corrupted

**Content looks wrong?**
- Regenerate the presentation
- Check your input data for accuracy
- Try with different business details
